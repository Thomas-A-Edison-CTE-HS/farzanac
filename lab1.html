About the Aurora Borealis

The Aurora Borealis

The Aurora Borealis, or Northern Lights, is a natural light display predominantly seen in high-latitude regions around the Artic.

How it happens

This phenonmeon occurs when charged particles from the sun collide with atoms in Earth's atmosphere, causing burst of light.

The Best Places to View

  • Norway
  • Sweden
  • Finland
  • Canada
  • Alaska, USA
