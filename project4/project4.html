In this game of, "Spot the Difference" there are 3 levels you can choose from depending on how difficult you'd like to play the game.

These 3 levels are:

  1. Easy
  2. Medium
  3. Hard

In each level you have only a few seconds to look at all of the objects. Then, Your screen turns black and turns back on shortly after.

Only 3 out of all the objects have changed. As you progress through each level, spotting these differences will only more difficult. Can you spot the difference?
